# Reload Workflow Command
This command is an instruction for you to redo your last steps.

## What to do
1. **Check your latest steps** - What have you started doing? What have you accomplished so far?
2. **Summarize your latest steps** - Summarize your latest steps in a clear and concise manner.
3. **Retry** - Retry your last steps, be it calling a subagent or running a command or anything else.
